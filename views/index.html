What is this?

When I was young, me and my older brother would play tournaments with our favorite fighting game.

We would write down the name of every character on a piece of paper, shuffle them, and alternately pick one at random. The one you had picked was your next contestant in the bracket.

Little by little, our tournament would fill up. By the end of it, we each owned half of the bracket.

The goal? Win the tournament with one of your characters.

Now here's a website to do just that.
